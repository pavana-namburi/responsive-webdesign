Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India & Former President

Life & Contributions

  • 1931 – Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu
  • 1960s – Worked with ISRO, helped launch SLV-III
  • 1990s – Led India’s nuclear tests and defense projects
  • 2002–2007 – Served as the 11th President of India
  • 2015 – Passed away while delivering a lecture
