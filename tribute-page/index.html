Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

"Missile Man of India" & 11th President of India

🌟 Life Journey

  • 1931 – Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu
  • 1960s – Joined ISRO; played a key role in India’s first satellite launch vehicle
  • 1980s–90s – Directed missile and defense programs (Agni, Prithvi)
  • 2002–2007 – Served as the 11th President of India
  • 2015 – Passed away while delivering a lecture at IIM Shillong
